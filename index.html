AWARDS

Awarded to the film that showcases the most impressive and groundbreaking visual effects in bringing Godzilla and other kaiju to life.

Acknowledging the visionary leadership behind the camera, the Best Director award goes to the individual who skillfully guides the film's narrative, tone, and overall direction.

This award is granted to a Godzilla movie that receives recognition at renowned film festivals. The Winner celebrates the film's ability to transcend the typical blockbuster genre on a global scale.

While Godzilla movies are known for their colossal creatures, the Best Actor award focuses on the human performances that contribute to the emotional depth and resonance of the film.
